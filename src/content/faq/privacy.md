question: "Why should you care about privacy when you have nothing to hide?"
short: "Privacy matters regardless of lawful behaviour."
---

You may not be making bombs, hiding weapons, selling illegal drugs or participating in any other unlawful activities at home, but that does not mean you are willing to let the police walk in and search your home at any time of night or day, without your permission, without a good legal reason, and without a search warrant.

The same logic applies to your data. The fact that we have nothing to hide is not a licence for the state, or for private companies, to monitor everything we do. Privacy is the precondition for free thought, dissent, and democratic participation.
