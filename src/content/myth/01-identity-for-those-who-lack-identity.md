---
order: 1
myth: "Aadhaar is identity for those who lack an identity."
fact: "To get Aadhaar, you need a Proof of Identity and a Proof of Address."
---

According to a Right to Information reply, only 0.03% of Aadhaar holders obtained it without showing two existing proofs of identity and address. The remaining 99.97% used existing identity cards.

Aadhaar does not, by itself, guarantee benefits like NREGA work, pensions, or ration foodgrains — separate eligibility conditions for these programmes continue to apply.
