order: 2
myth: "Aadhaar is a voluntary ID scheme. No services are linked to Aadhaar per se."
fact: "Aadhaar was sold to the public on the claim that enrolment was 'voluntary' — but in practice it has become mandatory."
---

Despite government claims in court and Parliament, Aadhaar is now demanded for scholarships, food grain and cooking gas subsidies, pensions, bank accounts, voting registration, and even marriage or adoption.

The system has made life without Aadhaar so difficult that the "voluntary" label is hollow — comparable to saying that breathing or eating is voluntary.
