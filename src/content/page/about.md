We are a non-partisan campaign concerned about the Unique Identification (UID) or Aadhaar project. The campaign shares relevant data and findings from the ground on the experience of citizens with the system.

If Aadhaar's promise was inclusion, its lived reality has been exclusion: from rations and pensions, from school admissions and bank accounts, from medical treatment and voting rolls. We document those exclusions, and we resist the further entrenchment of a coercive identity model.

**#No2UID · #AadhaarNahiSudhaarChaahiye**

## Code of Conduct

We have a zero-tolerance policy towards discrimination, harassment, bullying, or sexual harassment. The Code of Conduct applies to anyone participating in the campaign — members, volunteers, interns, and collaborators.

To report a violation, write to [contact@rethinkaadhaar.in](mailto:contact@rethinkaadhaar.in). Complaints are reviewed by the campaign's committee. Violations may result in removal from the campaign and loss of access to its channels.
